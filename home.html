About Us

Sharif Travel is a trusted bus transportation company providing safe, comfortable, and reliable travel services across Zanzibar. From Stone Town to various routes across the island, our goal is to connect people with ease and convenience through our modern bus network.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, affordable, and dependable bus travel services that ensure customer satisfaction while promoting safe and sustainable transportation in Zanzibar.

Our Vision

To become Zanzibar’s leading transport provider, recognized for innovation, efficiency, and exceptional service that connects communities and supports tourism growth.
